The Best Movies According to Mark Howson

My top three movies of all-time.

Rocky 2

This film is my all-time favourite. I think it is a wonderful'never give up' story of true grit and determination.
He is a tough guy with a soft heart. Here's Rockys poem to Adrian

A Poem For Rocky

Remember when we was on ice skates,
And I thought you was supposed to be great,
So I kept giving you lip,
And you kept trying to slip,
So I could catch you.
That was our first date,
And after that,
Every day was great.
That wherever you go,
So now I want you to know,
That wherever you go,
Atlantic City or in the snow,
Don’t worry about a thing,
’cause as long as I got this ring,
I’ll always be there to catch you.
~ Rocky’s poem to Adrian in Rocky II

Shawshank Redemption

An Awesome film which has some fantastic acting in(especially from Morgan Freeman). I like that the film has a message of hope.

Forest Gump

A superb film with comedy, sad, happy and serious story lines. I really enjoyed how ,although he struggles academically he seems to be amazing at everything
